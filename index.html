How Does It Work?

The Roundtable Chatroom event consists 2 sessions and is currently only being held online due to the ongoing pandemic, but we hope to host them in person soon. For the quality of discussion, there are limited spots on a first-come-first-serve basis, so we encourage you to sign up as soon as possible.

Session 1

1 hour of guided discussion by a mentor. In a group of 6-10 people, participants will rotate to a different table every 10 minutes. The exact duration of the first session depends on how many topics we have, which is usually 4-6.

Session 2

30 minutes of free discussion. Weâ€™ll reduce the group size to 3-5 people and shuffle the group every 10 minutes. There are no constraints on the topic to encourage interesting conversation.
